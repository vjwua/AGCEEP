#Fantasy Resurgant Granada 1.5.1 - Instructions

#-----------------------------------------------

made compatible with AGCEEP 1.59 again in 1.59b5
cut down the countries offered at start to just those in the iberian peninsula. Anyone else does not make sense because the Fantasy Granada scenario is in everything identical to the 1419 AGCEEP scenario with the exception of some events for Granada that an AI Granada will be unlikely to achieve. 

#-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Compatible with AGCEEP 1.56

Decompress the archive and copy resulting AGCEEP folder in EU2 main folder.

For Base users of AGCEEP, decompress resulting BaseGfx.rar and install in AGCEEP folder.
